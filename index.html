About me

What I do

I tell stories through visuals — crafting imagery, films, and creative direction that make people feel something.

Background

Based in India, I've been working as a visual storyteller for years — blending photography, film, and design into a singular creative vision.

Approach

I believe every frame should carry intention. I work closely with people to understand their story and translate it into something visually honest and lasting.
